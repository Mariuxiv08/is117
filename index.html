History & Mission

Latin American Student Organization (LASO) was founded in 1970 by a group of Latino students. The purpose of LASO is to expose the campus community of the Latin American influences in music, art, literature, history and cuisine as well as educational and sociopolitical concerns.Identifying undergraduate students and to those who desire to learn more about Latino community and culture. We strive to cultivate a sense of identity, community, and unity through campus outreach, greater-community involvement, and numerous other activities. We hope to foster understanding by sharing the culture and experience of people of Latin American descent at NJIT and beyond.
